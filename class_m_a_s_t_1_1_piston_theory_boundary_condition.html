const RealVectorX &  vel_vec

Constructor for the Piston Theory boundary condition object.

The arguments needed for initialization are order: order of piston theory, mach: mach number a_inf: ambient speed of sound, gamma: ratio of specific heats at constant pressure and constant volume, rho: ambient density for calculation of dynamic pressure, vel_vec: velocity unit vector
